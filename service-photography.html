FAQs

How long does a shoot typically take?

Shoot duration varies wildly based on scope. Simple headshots can take 1-2 hours, while comprehensive product catalogs or full-day events require custom scheduling.

How many photos will I receive?

This is determined during the planning phase. We prefer quality over quantity, delivering a precise selection of top-tier, fully retouched images as agreed upon.

Is retouching included?

Yes, all final delivered images include professional color correction and standard retouching to ensure a flawless finish.

Do you provide props or styling?

We have an assortment of standard studio props, but for specific brand styling (like unique food ingredients or lifestyle artifacts), we coordinate with you beforehand.

Do I get commercial usage rights?

Absolutely. For all corporate and commercial shoots, full usage rights are included so you can utilize the assets freely across ads, web, and print.

Do you travel for shoots?

Yes, we are based in Lahore but can travel nationally and internationally for assignments. Travel expenses are calculated transparently during quoting.
