Answers

FAQs

What file formats will I receive?

You will receive all standard industry formats. This typically includes vector files (AI, EPS, SVG) for scalability, as well as high-resolution raster formats (PNG, JPG, PDF) for web and print use.

Will I get brand guidelines?

Yes, comprehensive brand identity packages include a detailed brand guidelines document outlining logo usage, typography rules, color palettes, and visual tone to maintain consistency.

How many revision rounds are included?

Most of our packages include 2 to 3 dedicated revision rounds. We collaborate closely with you during the concept phase so the initial directions are highly aligned with your goals.

What is the timeline for a full branding project?

A full brand identity project generally takes 3 to 6 weeks from discovery to final delivery, depending on the complexity and how quickly we can exchange feedback.

Who owns the copyright to the designs?

Upon full payment, complete copyright and ownership of the final selected designs are transferred to you. We only retain the right to showcase the work in our portfolio.

Do you provide print-ready files?

Absolutely. We prepare print-ready PDFs with correct bleed, trim marks, and CMYK color profiles, ensuring your physical materials look just as vibrant as they do on screen.
