Answers

FAQs

What video formats do you deliver?

We deliver in all major formats depending on your needs: MP4, MOV, ProRes, and H.264/H.265. We also provide specific aspect ratios (16:9, 9:16, 1:1) optimized for various social platforms.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Short-form social media edits typically take 2-3 days, while longer brand films or complex motion graphics projects may take 1-3 weeks. We always agree on a clear delivery timeline upfront.

How do I send you my raw footage?

We use secure cloud storage platforms like Google Drive, Dropbox, or Frame.io. We'll provide you with a dedicated, organized folder where you can easily drop all your raw assets.

Do you handle music licensing?

Yes. We have subscriptions to premium royalty-free music and SFX libraries like Artlist and Epidemic Sound. Your final video will be fully cleared for commercial use without copyright issues.

Can you create different cuts for social media?

Absolutely. For any main video, we can provide bite-sized cutdowns optimized for Reels, TikTok, and YouTube Shorts, including native captions and fast-paced editing.

What is your revision policy?

We aim for perfection. We offer multiple rounds of revisions within the project scope to ensure the pacing, color, and storytelling precisely match your vision before the final sign-off.
