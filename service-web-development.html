FAQs

What tech stack do you use?

We primarily use modern, scalable technologies like Next.js, React, and Tailwind CSS for custom solutions, alongside robust platforms like WordPress and Shopify for CMS and e-commerce needs.

Do you provide hosting?

Yes, we can deploy your website on high-performance cloud platforms like Vercel or AWS, or assist in setting up your own preferred hosting environment.

What is the typical timeline?

A standard custom website usually takes between 4 to 8 weeks from initial planning to final launch, depending on the complexity and feature set required.

Can I manage content myself?

Absolutely. We build user-friendly backend dashboards using headless CMS or platforms like WordPress, empowering your team to update text and images easily.

Is SEO included?

Technical on-page SEO is built into the architecture of every site we develop, including proper semantic HTML, fast loading speeds, and responsive design.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes, we offer retainer packages for continuous support, regular updates, security monitoring, and ongoing optimization of your website.

Do you build e-commerce features?

Yes! We have extensive experience building scalable online stores using modern frameworks, Shopify, or WooCommerce, tailored to your specific product catalog.
